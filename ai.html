I'm working on a sci-fi novel and need help structuring the plot. Can you give me some tips on creating compelling story arcs?

2 min ago

Absolutely! Here are some key tips for structuring compelling sci-fi story arcs:

  1. Establish the World First: Build your universe with consistent rules before diving into plot.
  2. The Three-Act Structure: Setup (Act 1), Confrontation (Act 2), Resolution (Act 3).
  3. Character-Driven Conflict: Technology should serve human stories, not replace them.
  4. Foreshadowing: Plant seeds early that pay off in surprising ways later.
  5. Escalating Stakes: Each act should raise the stakes higher than the last.
